About Us

We Build Outdoor Spaces That Tell Your Story

At Rendered Outdoor Design, we believe the space outside your door should be just as thoughtful and livable as the space inside. We combine creative design with skilled craftsmanship to bring your vision to life.

Our team works closely with each client — listening, designing, refining — until the plan feels exactly right. Then we build it to last.

  • Custom designs tailored to your property and lifestyle
  • Licensed, insured, and locally rooted
  • Transparent pricing, no surprise costs
  • Full project management from concept to completion
